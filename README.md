Future City Simulator is an interactive web application that helps users explore how urban design decisions affect environmental sustainability. By adjusting parameters such as population, energy sources, and transportation systems, users can instantly see how their choices influence energy consumption, carbon emissions, and overall city health.

The project is designed to make complex ideas in urban planning and sustainability understandable through simple, visual simulation.
